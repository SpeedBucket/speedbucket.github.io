Quality Counts

The speed bucket out performs and out lasts all the other paint trays on the market.

Don't settle for inferior products. Don't buy a new paint tray every few weeks or months. I have used one of my speed buckets for over 15 years. Durable paint equipment will save you money!

Oops... If this has ever happened to you did you lose time or money cleaning up the mess? The Speed Bucket is a paint tray that is stable by design and hard to tip over. Avoiding problems saves you money.

Time Is Money

A Large paint roller will speed up your rolling time and as a result get you through the job faster.

The Speed Bucket is efficient for a 9 inch paint roller all the way up to the an 18 inch paint roller. I prefer to use the 14 inch roller set up when I have more than a gallon or two to paint. The 14 inch paint roller allows me to apply 50% more paint to the wall with the same number of roller strokes. The 18 inch roller applies 100% more paint than the 9 inch paint roller. The weight of a large paint roller is not a factor because most of the time a paint roller is used it is on the wall surface and the wall supports the weight.

The Speed Bucket is perfect for touch up painting. This paint tray carries like a suitcase as you walk to the areas you need to touch up. Because it is 24 inches wide and 14 inches deep I often paint small areas without a drop cloth. The Speed bucket catches the splatter from the paint roller.

The large pour spout on this paint tray allows you to clean up fast and easily. The Speed Bucket is made out of Polyethylene so the dry paint peals out easy.

200 Pound Test

The solid aluminum handle will never rust. It has been tested to hold over 200 pounds of paint and tools with out losing its shape.

Our production of the Speed Bucket is unique in that each paint tray takes over an hour at the factory to mold. By forming each paint tray slowly and with gradual heat, the product is more durable than any other plastic or tin tray on the market. The product is made from polyethylene. Forming the tray slowly, and using gradual heat with the polyethylene material makes the tray practically indestructible. We take pride in the creation and finished product of each Speed Bucket.

The Speed Bucket makes carrying ALL of these paint supplies onto the job site possible with one trip. Imagine all of the time and money you save with less trips to and from your truck or van.

John Carvey is the creator and manufacturer of the Speed Bucket paint tray. In 2002 the older versions of this paint tray for the 18 inch roller became hard to find in the Denver area. The painting supplies stores were having trouble getting this style of paint tray from the manufacturer. John took it upon himself to revise and improve the design, build a new mold and to produce his own line of trays. Throughout the years, this paint tray has saved him countless hours of time and money.
