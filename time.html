A Large paint roller will speed up your rolling time and as a result get you through the job faster.

The Speed Bucket is efficient for a 9 inch paint roller all the way up to the an 18 inch paint roller. I prefer to use the 14 inch roller set up when I have more than a gallon or two to paint.

The 14 inch paint roller allows me to apply 50% more paint to the wall with the same number of roller strokes.

The 18 inch roller applies 100% more paint than the 9 inch paint roller. The weight of a large paint roller is not a factor because most of the time a paint roller is used it is on the wall surface and the wall supports the weight.

The Speed Bucket is perfect for touch up painting.

This paint tray carries like a suitcase as you walk to the areas you need to touch up. Because it is 24 inches wide and 14 inches deep I often paint small areas without a drop cloth. The Speed bucket catches the splatter from the paint roller.

The large pour spout on this paint tray allows you to clean up fast and easily.

The Speed Bucket is made out of Polyethylene so the dry paint peals out easy.

Look for Speed Bucket where you buy your paint supplies!
