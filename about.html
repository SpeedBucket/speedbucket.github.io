John Carvey is the creator and manufacturer of this Speed Bucket paint tray. About five years ago the older versions of this paint tray for the 18 inch roller became hard to find in the Denver area. The painting supplies stores were having trouble getting this style of paint tray from the manufacturer. John took it upon himself to revise and improve the design, build a new mold and to produce his own line of trays. Throughout the years, this paint tray has saved him countless hours of time and money.

The Speed Bucket is a product of 10% Solution Inc. We contribute 10% or more of all profits to the following non-profit groups. This list is not inclusive. Contact us or the groups identified for more information.

Life Outreach International
They drill water wells for people who do not have clean drinking water. Life Today also distributes food and builds new homes for people in extreme poverty. www.lifetoday.org
World Vision
A Christian, humanitarian group that works with children, families, and communities worldwide. They focus on providing for the basic needs of the poor and solving the problems of poverty and injustice. www.worldvision.org
Prison Fellowship
Provides help for inmates and their families. Angel tree is a program they organize each year to deliver Christmas presents to the children of inmates. www.prisonfellowship.org
BCM
This group sends 100% of all money donated to help specific individuals and projects in the impoverished areas of South East Asia. Contact us for more information. Carvey123@comcast.net
